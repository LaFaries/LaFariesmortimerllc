Our story

Service is not a luxury. It never should have been.

LaFaries Mortimer LLC was founded with a clear purpose — to bridge the gap between the support that exists for large corporations and the support that small businesses, ministries, and working families actually have access to.

With 39 years of experience in administrative operations, IT systems, and business compliance, our founder built LaFaries Mortimer LLC not as a typical service business, but as a firm rooted in advocacy for the people who are most often underserved — first-time entrepreneurs, faith communities, and small business owners who are doing everything themselves.

Too many businesses fail not because the idea was bad, but because the administrative foundation was never built correctly. Missed deadlines, disorganized records, and compliance gaps quietly destroy businesses that had every reason to succeed.

We exist to change that — one business, one ministry, one family at a time.

"You should never feel alone when it comes to running your business. Behind every great organization is a structure that holds it up — we help you build that structure."

— LaFaries Mortimer LLC

The LAAP Suite™ story

One system to replace the chaos

After years of watching businesses struggle with the same preventable problems — missed annual report deadlines, disorganized formation documents, compliance gaps that appeared years after launch — it became clear that what was needed wasn't just another tool. What was needed was a complete operating system.

The LAAP Suite™ was built from the ground up to do exactly that. It brings together business formation, documentation, compliance tracking, annual maintenance, and administrative automation into one guided system — backed by nearly four decades of experience in what actually causes businesses to succeed or fail.

LAAP isn't software. It's a structure. And every business deserves one.
