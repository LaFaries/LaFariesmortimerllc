FAQ

Quick answers to the most common questions. We build tools and systems to bring clarity, order, and confidence to everyday work.

What does LaFaries Mortimer LLC do?

We provide administrative support, IT help, and system organization for small businesses, ministries, and families—so your work runs smoother and you feel confident.

Do I need to be “tech savvy” to work with you?

No. We keep things simple and walk with you step-by-step. If you can use email and click a link, you can use our tools.

What is LAAP Suite™?

LAAP Suite™ is a guided setup system that helps you organize your information and build your workflow step-by-step—without overwhelm.

How do I start?

Click “Book a Call” and tell us what you need help with. We’ll recommend the best next step.

Do you offer one-time help or monthly support?

Both. You can request a one-time setup, a project build, or ongoing support depending on your needs.

Do you build websites?

Yes—especially clean, simple websites that are easy to update and easy for your audience to use.

Where can I see your tools?

Visit the Apps & Portfolio page. Any tool that is live will have an “Open App” button.
