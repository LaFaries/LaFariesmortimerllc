Frequently Asked Questions

This page explains what LaFaries Mortimer LLC offers, how our free resources work, and when paid support may be helpful.

What is LaFaries Mortimer LLC?

LaFaries Mortimer LLC is a support-based service that provides free tutorials, downloadable templates, and affordable administrative and IT assistance. Our goal is to help individuals, small businesses, and ministries get organized and move forward without unnecessary costs.

Do you really offer free resources?

Yes. We intentionally provide free tutorials and guides as a way to give back. These resources show you step by step how to complete common tasks on your own.

What kinds of tutorials do you provide?

Tutorials cover real-world topics such as basic IT setup, cybersecurity awareness, file organization, administrative workflows, EIN guidance, LLC basics, meeting documentation, and modern office tools.

Are all services paid?

No. Tutorials and learning resources are free. Paid services are optional and are for those who want hands-on, personalized assistance or prefer not to do everything themselves.

Who is this service for?

This service is for entrepreneurs, ministries, nonprofits, families, and individuals who want clarity and structure without being overwhelmed or overcharged.

Do you provide legal, tax, or financial advice?

No. We do not replace licensed professionals. Our role is educational and administrative support, helping you understand processes and stay organized.

How do I know if I need paid support?

If you’re comfortable following a guide, the free tutorials may be enough. If you need help applying the steps, organizing systems, or troubleshooting, paid support may be helpful.

Ready to Get Started?

Begin with the free tutorials, then explore templates or reach out if you need help.
