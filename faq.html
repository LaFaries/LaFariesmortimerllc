LAAP Suite™ covers:

Annual Maintenance
What is Annual Maintenance?

Annual Maintenance is our ongoing compliance support service. We track your state and federal filing deadlines, send you reminders before they're due, provide filing guidance, and keep your business documents organized — so you never miss a deadline or fall out of good standing.

Which states does Annual Maintenance cover?

All 50 states. Every state has different annual report requirements, due dates, and fees. We track the specific requirements for your state and notify you in advance.

What happens if I miss an annual report deadline?

Missing a deadline can result in late fees, penalties, and in some states your LLC can be administratively dissolved — meaning it loses its good standing and legal protections. Annual Maintenance is specifically designed to prevent this.

Is Annual Maintenance included in LAAP Suite™?

Yes — the Standard tier of Annual Maintenance is included in LAAP Suite™ subscriptions. It is also available as a standalone service. See our Annual Maintenance page for pricing details.

Does Annual Maintenance apply to ministries and nonprofits?

Yes. Ministries and nonprofits have unique filing requirements — annual filings, 501(c)(3) maintenance, state charitable registrations. Our Annual Maintenance service covers these requirements and keeps your organization in good standing.

Business formation
Can you help me form an LLC?

Yes. We provide guided LLC formation support — walking you through choosing a business name, filing articles of organization, obtaining your EIN, setting up an operating agreement, and opening a business bank account. This is a core part of the LAAP Suite™.

What states can you help me form an LLC in?

All 50 states. While we are based in Miami, Florida, we assist clients with LLC formation in every state.

What's the difference between an LLC and a nonprofit?

An LLC is a for-profit business structure that provides personal liability protection and flexible tax treatment. A nonprofit is a tax-exempt organization formed to serve a public or charitable purpose — it cannot distribute profits to members. Ministries and churches typically operate as nonprofits. We assist with both structures.

Can you help me get an EIN?

Yes. We provide step-by-step EIN application guidance. An EIN is required for opening a business bank account, hiring employees, filing taxes, and many other business activities. Our free tutorials include an EIN walkthrough, and LAAP Suite™ includes hands-on EIN support.
