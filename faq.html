Frequently Asked Questions

How LaFaries Mortimer LLC Can Help You

FAQ

These questions are based on what clients like you often ask after visiting the website. If you still have questions after reading through this section, you can always reach out directly for clarity or a one-on-one consultation.

What types of services do you offer?

+

I offer a blend of administrative support, IT and technical assistance, business and nonprofit formation guidance, and self-service tools such as tutorials and templates. You can choose to work independently using the free resources, or request hands-on help when you need someone to step in and handle the details with you.

What makes LaFaries Mortimer LLC different from other administrative or IT services?

+

Many people are overcharged or taken advantage of when they need basic administrative or technology help. LaFaries Mortimer LLC was created out of a desire to serve with integrity, care, and fairness. My focus is on education, affordability, and empowerment — not just doing things for you, but helping you understand the process so you can move forward with confidence.

What is the LAAP System and how will it help me?

+

LAAP (LaFaries Administration Automation Platform) is a guided system that helps you organize your administrative work, generate documents, follow clear workflows, and understand important steps in your business or ministry setup. It is designed for small businesses, ministries, and nonprofits who need a simple way to bring order to tasks, documents, and responsibilities without hiring a full-time administrator or IT team.

Do I need to be "tech-savvy" to use your tools or the LAAP system?

+

No. Everything I build is created with beginners and non-technical users in mind. If you can click through simple screens and follow prompts, you will be able to use the tools. When needed, I can also provide one-on-one support to walk you through steps such as using templates, organizing files, or completing online applications.

Are your tutorials and templates really free?

+

Yes. The self-help tutorials and many foundational templates are offered as a free resource library so you can move forward without pressure. These include guides for EIN, D-U-N-S, LLC basics, meeting minutes, IT fundamentals, and more. You only pay when you decide you want personalized services or a deeper level of support.

When should I move from self-service to paid support?

+

You may want to request paid services when:

  • You're unsure about filing documents correctly
  • You don't have time to set up systems yourself
  • You want things done for you, not just explained
  • You're preparing for banks, grants, or board reviews
  • You need ongoing support, not just one-time instructions

Do you help with business or nonprofit formation (LLC, church, ministry, etc.)?

+

Yes. I can guide you through EIN applications, D-U-N-S setup, basic LLC or nonprofit formation steps, and help you organize the documents you'll need for banks, vendors, and internal records. You will understand what you're doing and why, instead of just signing forms in the dark.

Do you work with ministries and churches?

+

Absolutely. Many ministries need help with meeting minutes, event structure, record-keeping, digital organization, and simple IT systems. I understand church and nonprofit environments and can help you create clear, respectful, and organized systems that support your calling.

Can you help with computer or email issues and basic technology setup?

+

Yes. IT support can include setting up computers, helping with email accounts, organizing cloud storage, strengthening passwords, and walking you through video meeting tools like Zoom or Teams. You don't need to be "good with computers" — that's what I'm here for.

Do you only serve clients in Florida?

+

No. While I am based in Miami, Florida, I work with clients virtually across different locations. Many services, templates, tutorials, and guidance sessions are conducted online via phone, email, or video.

How do I contact you or schedule a consultation?

+

You can contact me directly by phone at (305) 859-1279, by email at info@lafariesmortimerllc.com, or by using the contact form on the website to share your goals. From there, we can schedule a consultation and decide what level of support makes sense for your season and budget.

How quickly will I hear back after I submit a form or send an email?

+

Most inquiries receive a response within 24–48 business hours, Monday through Friday. If your request is time-sensitive, feel free to also call so we can address it as quickly as possible.

Are payment plans or phased projects available?

+

For certain services—especially larger system setups or ongoing administrative support—payment plans or phased projects may be available. We can talk through options during your consultation so you're not overwhelmed by cost or pace.

Is my information kept private and secure?

+

Yes. Your information, documents, and business details are treated with confidentiality and care. I do not share your information with outside parties, and I only request the details needed to properly serve you.

I'm a first-time entrepreneur and feel overwhelmed. Is this still for me?

+

Yes—this work was designed with you in mind. Many of my clients are first-time founders, ministry leaders, or small business owners who simply need structure, clarity, and a trusted guide. We will move at a practical pace, and you will not be made to feel behind, rushed, or inadequate.

I'm not sure what I need yet—can I still reach out?

+

Absolutely. Many people come with a feeling of "I know I need help, but I don't know where to start." That's normal. During your consultation, we'll talk through your current season, identify the most urgent gaps, and decide together whether self-service tools, one-time setup, or ongoing support is the best fit.
