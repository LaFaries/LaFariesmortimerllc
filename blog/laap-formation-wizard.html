Here is an overview of what the formation sequence covers:

01
Organizational Identity
Establish your business name, entity type, purpose statement, and core operational identity.
02
Legal Formation & Filing
State registration, Articles of Organization or Incorporation, and foundational compliance documentation.
03
Federal Identification
EIN acquisition, federal tax setup, and identification structure for banking and operations.
04
Banking & Financial Infrastructure
Business banking setup, payment processing, invoicing systems, and financial separation guidance.
05
Digital Presence & Branding
Domain, website, branded email, Google Business Profile, and digital identity establishment.
06
Operational Documentation
Operating agreements, bylaws, internal policies, and foundational organizational documents.
07
Communication & CRM Systems
Contact management, lead tracking, scheduling infrastructure, and client communication setup.
08
Compliance & Maintenance Planning
Annual report calendars, renewal tracking, compliance dashboards, and proactive deadline management.
09
Workflow Automation
AI agent assignment, automation setup, and operational workflow configuration.
10
Legacy & Continuity Planning
Succession documentation, organizational preservation, and long-term sustainability framework.
