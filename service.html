What We Do on the Services Side

Think of this as your professional support layer — when you're ready for someone to walk with you through the details, not just give you a template.

Business & Nonprofit Formation

Guidance and structure so you don't guess your way through forming an LLC, ministry, or nonprofit.

  • LLC / nonprofit formation support (Florida-focused, wider application)
  • Sunbiz navigation, name checks, and formation outline
  • EIN application guidance (what to enter and where)
  • Bank-ready documentation support

Administrative Systems & Operations

Bring order to your office, documents, meetings, and team workflows so you can operate like the leader you are.

  • Digital file organization and naming structure (2025 system)
  • Meeting agendas, minutes, and decision tracking
  • Simple SOPs for recurring tasks and key processes
  • Board documentation and compliance

IT Support for Everyday Users

Non-technical, practical support for setting up your digital foundation without overwhelm or tech jargon.

  • New computer setup for work or ministry
  • Password managers and 2-step verification basics
  • Cloud storage setup (Google Drive / OneDrive)
  • Zoom / Teams setup, audio/video troubleshooting
